The Iridescent Majesty of
Morpho Helena

Widely heralded as the crown jewel of lepidopterology, the Morpho Helena showcases structural coloration of unparalleled brilliance. Preserved with flawless museum-grade care in our London studio, its metallic blue wings shimmer dynamically in response to light, making it a highly coveted masterpiece for the modern sanctuary.
