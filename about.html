SIGNUP

Art Through Evolution

Founded in 2012 • London

The Studio Vision

What started as a private collection in a small Kensington attic has grown into one of the world's leading insect taxidermy studios. Our goal has always been to bridge the gap between biological science and fine art, presenting nature's most intricate designs in a way that demands contemplation and respect.

Every wing, every mandible, and every iridescent scale is handled with the utmost care, ensuring that the specimen's natural brilliance is preserved for lifetimes to come.

Commitment to Ethics

Conservation

We only source from butterfly farms and conservation projects that actively work to prevent habitat loss.

Fair Trade

Supporting local indigenous communities in rainforest areas by providing sustainable economic alternatives.

Legal Compliance

All specimens are accompanied by CITES permits where required, ensuring full international legal compliance.

The Founder

Elias Thorne

"Nature is the greatest artist; I am merely its humble curator. My goal is to freeze a moment of perfection so that its beauty can be studied and admired long after the biological cycle has concluded."

Elias holds a Master's degree in Entomology and has worked with some of the world's leading museums to preserve rare insect collections. His unique artistic approach has earned the studio international acclaim.

The Creative Journey

Discovery

We work with a global network of conservationists to find naturally expired specimens that meet our strict quality standards.

Preparation

Using specialized hydration chambers, we restore flexibility to the specimen before hand-pinning into the perfect posture.

Common Enquiries

How are the insects sourced? +

We source exclusively from sustainable butterfly farms and conservation projects. These programs support local ecosystems by giving native populations a financial incentive to preserve the rainforest rather than clear it for agriculture.

Do they require special maintenance? +

Our frames are sealed with museum-grade UV protection glass. Simply keep them out of direct sunlight and in a stable temperature environment to prevent fading or deterioration.

Can I request a specific species? +

Yes, we handle custom commissions. If you have a specific species in mind, contact our studio and we will check our archives or source it ethically through our network.
